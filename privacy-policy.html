Privacy Policy

1.1 In order to provide a better service to you, we may collect and process the following data about you:

1.1.1 Information that you provide to us by filling in forms on our site www.propercredit.com.my and on Mobile Application (hereinafter referred as “our site”), in person or through other forms of communication. This includes information provided at the time of registering to use our site, applying for loan and related services or requesting further services. We may also ask you for information at other times, for example in processing the loan application, extending or renewal of loan application or other services, in connection with a promotion or when you report a problem with our site;

1.1.2 Personal data that we may collect includes but is not limited to:

  • Personal identification information – such as your full name, national identification number, email address, phone number, gender, education level, marital status, ethnicity, place of residence, employment status, industry of employment and salary payment method that would allow us to contact you personally and as part of our Customer Identification / EKYC process;
  • Financial information and documentation – such as salary of your employment, copies of a proof of your income or net worth;
  • Account login information – such as your phone number and password, including the information collected through your Facebook, Google or other social media accounts which have been linked to the account on the Website and Mobile Application;
  • National identity documentation – such as a copy of your national identity document, proof of residence such as and not limited to electricity and / or water bill, and/or other information deemed necessary to comply with our legal obligations under applicable law;
  • Photos or documents – to make your application process easier, we may collect the documents as abovementioned (but is not limited to) as part of our verification and EKYC process;
  • App usage and device information – such as type of operating system, device model, information about how you use or navigate our applications including which pages you view and for how long, and other similar information and / or statistics such as date and time of visit to provide us some insights to improve the user experience;

1.1.7 Personal data that may be obtained from credit reporting agency (whose contact details can be provided upon request) or from the public domain through different channels, e.g. a public register, a public search engine, a public directory or any social networks etc. Device information may be collected from your web browsers.

1.2 It is necessary for us to collect and process your personal data. Failure to supply such personal data or consent to this privacy policy may result in Proper Credit being unable to process your applications for loan facilities or related services, open or continue accounts. We only retain personal data for so long as it is necessary. Data may be archived as long as the purpose for which the data was used still exists
